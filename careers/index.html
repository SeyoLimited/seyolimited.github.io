Why join Seyo?

Our mission is to unify the way robotic fleets are developed for, deployed and scaled.

Robotics have evolved beyond manufacturing innovations, with our belief being that the next leap for robotics will come from software

We are at the beginning of this journey, and the path ahead is an exciting one with opportunity to face challenges, develop and grow, and set standards for the industry.

Our culture is fun, ambitious and open, everyone is valued and no opinion goes unheard

We aim high, and so are always looking for innovators, risk takers and bright people so that we can not only aim high, but hit high too

Our Values

Results matter, not hours

Results = Progress. We do not ask for overtime, we do not ask for you to clock-in or clock-out, as long as your deliver, everyone is happy.

Think outside the box, but don’t lose sight of your goals

Developing innovative solutions requires lateral thought, but we have to stick to our values and goals when stepping through the fog-of-war

Be frank and constructive

There is nothing that worse in a fast-growing team then yes-people. We want people to be frank and challenge bad ideas with more ideas.

You’re not just an employee

You own part of the company. This means our success is your success. Our revenue is your revenue.
