QA Engineer

ENQA0001

Posted: November 2, 2011

Role

As a QA Engineer, you will be working with a passionate group of cross-disciplinary engineers to design and develop test suites involving all the software and hardware components of the Seyo platform. You will be creating state-of-art testing solutions that bring robots to life and ensure their reliability. You will be able to see the results of your work both in a 3D simulation environment and in a live testing warehouse where real robots will operate according to your instructions.

You will learn how to automate your test cases and use containerisation technologies to bring test reproducibility to the next level.

If you always dreamed about seeing the direct impact of your work in the real world and are an avid learner, this may be the right place for you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, document and maintain end-to-end test suites.
  • Develop testing strategies that exercise the system under test in both simulated and physical environments.
  • Investigate and propose technical and technological solutions for testing.
  • Participate in interviews for new hires in engineering, with a focus on QA and testing roles.
  • Participate in stand-ups, planning sessions and architectural meetings.
  • Support colleagues during troubleshooting sessions.
  • Conduct and participate in code reviews.
  • Mentor junior engineers with a focus on QA and testing related techniques and technologies.

Experience

  • 3+ years of experience with black-box/white-box testing.
  • Good understanding of API testing principles.
  • Experience with execution of smoke tests and system releases.
  • Experience with Docker is highly desirable, but not necessary.
  • Experience in ROS, Gazebo or robotics testing is a plus.

About Us

Our core technology enables heterogeneous fleets of robots to cooperate on complex tasks even when they lose access to a central network. We focus on logistics automation to solve the fragmentation of robotic solutions in the industry, which is the main obstacle towards adoption amongst SMEs. By enabling automation across the logistics network, we create a more resilient, distributed and diverse backbone for the delivery of supplies. You will be joining an exciting venture-backed startup on the ground floor, building the foundations for the company culture, structure and products.
